I’m Attending AEA Seattle!

Published April 2, 2010 by Rob Glazebrook.

Once I’m done stuffing myself with Easter-related goodies this Sunday, I’ll be catching a plane to Seattle to attend the An Event Apart conference.

Some of you probably haven’t heard of An Event Apart. I’ll give you a hint: if you’re in to web design (and if you’re here, I really hope you are), the conference title is a spot-on description. An Event Apart is two days of presentations on web design, and the evolution and mastery thereof, hosted by those Grecian gods of web design over at Happy Cog. It’s my favorite conference to date… though I admittedly haven’t made it to SXSW Interactive yet.

Some of you might be attending the conference as well. If so, I’d love to meet up with you, chat, share a drink, whatever. I’m planning to attend both days of the conference (obviously), both after-parties (also obviously), and the 1-day workshop on day 3.

If anyone would like to meet up, the easiest way to coordinate would probably be via Twitter, so follow me and let me know you’ll be at An Event Apart next week, and I’ll follow right back! Leaving a comment here as well might also let others in on the fun, if fun indeed is to be had.

Have a great week, everyone!
