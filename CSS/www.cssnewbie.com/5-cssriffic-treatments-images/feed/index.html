]]> By: Bernhard Schulte http://www.cssnewbie.com/5-cssriffic-treatments-images/comment-page-1/#comment-48 Thu, 14 Feb 2008 16:25:30 +0000 http://www.cssnewbie.com/css-rules/5-cssriffic-treatments-images/#comment-48 The depth treatment can be simplified by using “inset” or “outset” as border attributes. Saves you three lines of typing.

Your example would then read:
img.deep {
background-color:#fcfcfc;
padding:4px;
border:1px solid #ccc outset;
}

Important when using inset or outset: Always choose a relatively light (i.e. non-black) border color, or the effect may not show in some browsers.
